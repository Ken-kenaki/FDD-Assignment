Essential Macronutrients

Building blocks for muscle repair and growth. Aim for 0.8-1.2g per kg of body weight daily.

Carbohydrates

Primary energy source for your body and brain. Focus on complex carbs for sustained energy.

Plan Ahead

Prepare meals in advance to maintain healthy eating habits.

Use the plate method: 1/2 vegetables, 1/4 protein, 1/4 grains.

Mindful Eating

Eat slowly and pay attention to hunger and fullness cues.
