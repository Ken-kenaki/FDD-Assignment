Fitness & Strength

Build strength, mobility, and conditioning with a proven 12‑week plan and coaching cues.

Overview

Three structured sessions per week with video demos, tempo, and RPE guidance. Deload weeks maintain momentum while protecting recovery.

text

Weekly Structure

  • Day A: Squat pattern, push, row
  • Day B: Hinge pattern, press, pull‑down
  • Day C: Split squat, chest press, core

Equipment

Dumbbells or kettlebells are sufficient to start. Barbell options are provided for those with access.

What You’ll Gain

Improved strength, posture, and confidence—plus sustainable training habits that fit a busy week.

Enroll

12 weeks - Lifetime updates
